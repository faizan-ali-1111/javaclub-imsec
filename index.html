About Java Club

Department of Computer Science and Engineering This is noticeable that some subjects of B.Tech course curriculum are very vital and useful. These subjects are currently demanded by the industry and industry wants that students should be skilled in these technologies, one of these technology is Java/J2EE/Html/CSS/JavaScript. The Java/J2EE technology is very vast which requires few months to become someone familiar with the technology. Since the technology is so vast, therefore there is a requirement to do something additional to continue the study, problems solving, discussion about important aspects and practice of some important exercises of the technology beyond the class room also. Keeping the above points in mind the Department of CSE, IMSEC Ghaziabad has formed JAVA CLUB to carry out all the above mentioned activities about said technology.

The objective of the java club is the technological enhancement the students in Java Technology which includes, question/answers of java technology, puzzles of java, practical applications of Java etc.

Following activities are to be carried out in the club:-

1: Conducting classes on weekend (4 to 6 hours )(Session Video & Notes will be provided)

2: Preparing students for java mock test for industrial recruitment.

3: Conducting of programming contest.

4: Alumni or Industry Expert interaction with the members.

5: Mini Project development on Java technology and web base application.

6: All Course are free of cost


The club is driven by the students of B.Tech -CSE and it is supported by CSE faculty members. The activities of the club are initiated after discussion among faculty members and core club students members then it is executed by the core club members of java club.
